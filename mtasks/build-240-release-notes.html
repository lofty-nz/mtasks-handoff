b240 Postpone time blocks per day + day-calendar explained (#51 comment)
  • Calendar time blocks (Sleep, self-care, meals, work \u2014 the routine on the top lane) can now be postponed for a single day. Tap a block on the Day view and the modal shows \u23f3 Postpone \u2014 just <date> (+30m / +1h / +2h / +3h / +4h, with \u21ba reset). It shifts only that day \u2014 e.g. you went to bed late \u2014 and never changes the recurring time. The shift is stored as block.shifts[date]; a shifted block shows \u23f3 on its bar, the new time flows through both lanes and the free-hours total, and an overnight block (Sleep 22:30\u201306:30) carries its shift into the next morning correctly.
  • For the question in the comment \u2014 the day calendar has two lanes on purpose: the top lane is your non-negotiable blocks (the routine), the bottom lane is your scheduled tasks (dashed bars you drag / tick / \u00d7). Keeping them separate means a task booked over a block reads clearly instead of overlapping it.
